**Terminator**: Represented by a rounded rectangle, the terminator symbol indicates the start or end of a process.

---

**Flow Lines**: Represented by arrows, the flow lines indicate the direction and sequence of the steps in the algorithm.

---

**Input/Output**: Represented by a parallelogram, the input/output symbol represents the points where data enters or leaves the system.

---

**Process**: Represented by a rectangle, the process symbol denotes a step or action within the algorithm.

---

**Decision**: Represented by a diamond shape, the decision symbol indicates a branching point where the flow of the algorithm depends on a specific condition or choice.
